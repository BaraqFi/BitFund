About Us

BitFund Solutions is a subsidiary of BitFund Solutions Limited, under the parental control of CPL Resources. The company is registered with company number 01040440, we are licensed and authorized by the appropriate authorities to conduct business in the various trade markets.

BitFund Solutions is a robust trade company that focus its operations in the forex market, as well as the cryptocurrency, binary, stock and bond market.

Our mission is to simplify the lengthy proceedings involved in trading, also to minimize risks through the aid of our artificial intelligence trading robot, thereby enabling both newbie and veteran investors to earn from the various trade markets without the need to acquire trading knowledge or deploy experience.

Our vision is long term, and is backed by our involvement and investment in the various trade markets. The profits generated from these investments are channeled towards enhancing our service delivery, and promoting our growth stability.

BitFund Solutions encompasses of professional traders and market analysts with over 6 to 10 years experience. Our management and customer service team are top notch and available to be of service to clients round the clock.

We provide our clients with support and advanced technology, designed to help them earn better and transform their dreams into achievements. BitFund Solutions business model combines assisted trading systems, with algorithms in the forex market, cryptocurrency, binary, stock and bond market. Thus, creating a unique and lasting experience for our clients.

We are building a reputation as one of the leading companies in online trade and investment, through our sheer commitment to helping our clients prevent losses, while increasing their profits. Our policies, skills and expertise are dedicated to helping you identify and eliminate hinderances to your financial growth.
